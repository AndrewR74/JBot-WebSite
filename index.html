Overview

A completely free, cross-platform(Mac, Windows, Linux), standalone Trivia - Riddle - Jeopardy bot for any Twitch channel. Allow your chat to compete for top score by playing various games JBot offers for free.

Game Commands

JBot will enhance your channels chat experience with just a click of a button. JBot is a cross platform extension that just works with no configuring needed. Although, there is no initial configuration is needed; JBot can be easily configured to meet your channels needs through the use of its extensive list of game commands.

Community Built Modules

JBot has the capability to expand its functionality by using modules. Anyone can develop a module using JavaScript.
